Our Team

Meet Our Caring Team

Alice Morgan, Veterinarian

Dr. Alice Morgan is a 2004 graduate of the Ontario Veterinary College at the University of Guelph. She joined our team of veterinarians in 2010.

Holly Jackson, Veterinarian

Dr. Holly Jackson is a native of Ottawa, and joined our clinic in 2012. Dr. Jackson attended the University of Ottawa for her Doctorate of Veterinary Medicine.

Martha Morris, Veterinarian

Dr. Martha Morris is a 1998 graduate of University of Calgary's College of Veterinary Medicine. After graduation, Dr. Morris did veterinary work in Toronto before joining the team in 2009.

Jessica Hall, Veterinarian

Dr. Jessica Hall is an Ottawa native who previously worked at our clinic as a veterinary assistant in high school. After receiving her Doctorate of Veterinary Medicine from the University of Carleton, she rejoined the team in 2013.
